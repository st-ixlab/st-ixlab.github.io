Seoultech IXLab

The Interaction Lab (IXLAB) is a dedicated HCI research group focused on redefining the boundaries between humans and technology. We strive to create intelligent, context-aware systems that seamlessly integrate into everyday life, leveraging advanced interaction technologies to transform how individuals perceive, interact with, and find meaning in an increasingly blended digital-physical world.

Research Pillars

Intelligent Interaction

Developing context-aware systems that understand and adapt to user behavior in real-time.

Mixed Reality

Bridging the physical and digital worlds through immersive AR/VR experiences.

Human-AI Collaboration

Designing interfaces that facilitate seamless collaboration between humans and artificial intelligence.

Social Computing

Exploring how technology can enhance social connection and communication.
